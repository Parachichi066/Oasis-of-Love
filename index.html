About Us

Oasis of Love (O.O.L.) is a non-profit organization aimed at improving individual and social well-being for all members of a community. This organization leans closer towards the use of compassion to act as a fountain for humanitarian acts; it advocates for the unity of all members of society and the need to help the ones suffering in the community so as to build a more aware and accepting society.

Vision

To make Oasis of Love a fountain for humanitarian acts so as to improve community welfare.
